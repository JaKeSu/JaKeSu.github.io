1.单机版hadoop

cd opt/hadoop-2.6.4/etc/hadoop/
vi hadoop-env.sh 文件末尾追加：export JAVA_HOME=/opt/jdk1.8.0_101

2.伪分布式搭建

cd /opt/hadoop-2.6.4/etc/hadoop/
vi core-site.xml

<configuration>
        <property>
                <name>fs:defaultFS</name>
                <value>hdfs://master:9000</value>
        </property>
</configuration>

vi hdfs-site.xml 

<configuration>
        <property>
                <name>dfs.replication</name>
                <value>1</value>
        </property>

</configuration>

hdfs namenode -format
:set nember显示行数
:22跳转到22行

/opt/hadoop-2.6.4/sbin/start-dfs.sh
jps 

2134 NameNode
2248 DataNode
2393 SecondaryNameNode

浏览器中输入master的ip和端口号
查看dataNodes下是否有一个叫slave0的奴隶
